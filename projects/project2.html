Emojipedia

The emojipedia project is a visually engaging React.js application that presents a collection of emojis with their meanings. Users can explore and understand various emojis through an intuitive and user-friendly interface. The project utilizes React.js to dynamically generate and display emoji entries. Each entry consists of an emoji, its name, and a description, providing an informative and enjoyable experience. The project demonstrates the versatility of React.js in creating interactive and visually appealing applications. With room for expansion, the emojipedia project has the potential to incorporate additional features, enhancing the overall user experience.

Technologies used: HTML,CSS,REACT.JS
